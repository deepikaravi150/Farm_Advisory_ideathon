# 🌾 FarmAdvisor — 7-Minute Demo Script
### Bharat-VISTAAR Integration Agent · Personalised AI Farm Advisory

> **Format:** 15-min slot → **7 min live demo** (this script) + ~1 min buffer + **7 min Q&A**
> **Goal:** Strong first impression, one clean narrative thread (a real farmer's day), every feature mapped to a real problem we solve.
> **Setup before you start:** Be logged in as a demo farmer who already has 1 active crop plan, a soil report uploaded, and land drawn. Have the language switcher and a mic ready. Browser zoomed so widgets are readable.

## 0:00 – 0:45 · THE HOOK 🎯
*(Don't open the laptop yet. Look at the room. Slow down.)*

> "India has **140 million farming households**. For every **1,000 farmers**, there is roughly **one** agriculture extension officer. So the question isn't whether good agronomic advice exists — it does, in ICAR and the KVKs. The question is: **does it ever reach the one farmer standing in his field this morning, deciding whether to irrigate today or wait for rain?**
>
> Today, for almost all of them, the answer is no. They ask a neighbour, or the input dealer who profits from selling them more fertiliser. A wrong call — wrong crop, wrong timing, one missed pest — and a season is gone. That is the quiet engine behind **10,000+ farmer suicides every year**.
>
> We built **FarmAdvisor** — a personal agronomist in every farmer's pocket, in their own language. Let me show you a single morning in a farmer's life."

*(Now turn to the screen.)*

---

## 0:45 – 1:30 · WHO THE FARMER IS 👤
*(Open the app on the landing page, then go to the dashboard. If smooth, briefly show the registration screen first.)*

> "This isn't a generic chatbot. It starts from **identity**. The farmer signs in with their **AgriStack Farmer ID** — the same 7.63 crore-ID system the government has already built — verified by **OTP**."

*(Point to the land map on the registration/dashboard.)*

> "At sign-up they **draw their land boundary on the map** and pick their language — English, हिन्दी, or தமிழ். From that one ID, the app already knows their **parcel, location, soil, and crop history**. Everything you'll see next is personalised to *this* farmer's field — not a textbook average."

**👉 Problem solved:** *Personalisation at scale — the same data foundation the Ministry is building, turned into something a single farmer can use.*

---

## 1:30 – 3:15 · THE MORNING DASHBOARD ☀️
*(Land on the dashboard. Read the greeting + date aloud — it feels alive.)*

> "Every morning the farmer opens this. The very first thing — before anything else — is **'Today's Plan'**."

*(Point to the colored **Today Action Banner** at the top.)*

> "This banner fuses two live signals: their **active crop plan stage** and the **real-time weather forecast** for their exact coordinates. So it doesn't say 'it might rain' — it says *'Today on your Chilli: flowering stage — thunderstorm likely, hold off spraying and keep drainage open.'* One sentence, one action."

*(Scroll to the **Weather widget**.)*

> "Below it, the full picture — current conditions plus a **16-day forecast**, pulled live from Open-Meteo for their land centre."

*(Point to the **Market Prices widget** — this is a money moment, linger here.)*

> "Now this is where we attack one of the most brutal problems in Indian farming — **price collapse**. When everyone plants the same crop and sells the same week, prices crash and produce gets dumped. Here we pull **live mandi prices from Agmarknet / data.gov.in** for their crop — average modal price, and a **trend arrow: rising, falling, or steady**. That single arrow is the difference between *sell now* and *hold two weeks*."

*(Quickly sweep across the remaining widgets so they register the breadth.)*

> "And it's all here together — **soil health** from their lab report, **suitable crops** for their land, their **land map**, and on the right, their **advisor chat**. One screen, the whole farm."

**👉 Problems solved:** *Planning confidence (they know what today demands) + market timing (sell signals to prevent distress dumping).*

## 3:15 – 5:00 · THE AI CROP PLAN — THE HEART 🌱
*(Open the Crop Plan page. This is your centrepiece — give it room.)*

> "Most farmers have **no season plan at all**. Decisions are reactive — they fight a pest *after* the damage, fertilise by habit, irrigate on a fixed schedule. We replace that with a forward-looking plan."

*(Open 'New Plan' → the State Assessment modal. Show the three states without dwelling.)*

> "We meet the farmer wherever they are: **'I don't know what to plant'**, **'I know my crop'**, or **'I'm already mid-season.'** A few quick questions — experience, water source, past issues — and the AI does the rest."

*(Show the generated timeline — the date-ranged stages.)*

> "What comes back isn't a paragraph of advice. It's a **project-management timeline for the whole season** — every stage date-ranged from sowing to harvest, with **per-stage tasks, costs, a total budget estimate, and a sell window.** The farmer can finally take a loan with a plan in hand instead of a hope."

*(Point to the **Current Stage** card + the input/water/pesticide breakdown.)*

> "It always highlights the **current stage** with a **risk level**, and tells them exactly what the crop needs *right now* — water, and critically, **inputs only when actually needed.** No 'spray pesticide unless you see symptoms.' That alone cuts the fertiliser and pesticide over-spend that drives farmers into debt."

*(Now the wow moment — the **Plan Assistant chat** on the right.)*

> "And the plan is a **conversation**, not a PDF. Watch —"

*(Type or speak into the Plan Assistant, e.g.:)* **"Shift my sowing one week later and lower the budget."**

> "It understands, **previews the change**, and only applies it when the farmer confirms. The plan adapts in real time — as weather shifts, as the farmer changes his mind."

**👉 Problems solved:** *Season-long planning + confidence to invest + cutting input-cost waste and debt.*

## 5:00 – 6:10 · ASK THE ADVISOR 🎙️
*(Go to the Farm Advisor chat. This proves it's a real agronomist, not a script.)*

> "Now the farmer just has a question — like he'd ask an expert he can't afford. And he doesn't type. He **talks.**"

*(Click the mic. Speak a real question, ideally switch the language first to தமிழ் or हिन्दी for impact:)*
**"My chilli leaves are curling at the edges — what should I do?"**

*(Let the voice transcribe, answer appears, then play it back with voice output.)*

> "Speech-to-text, an answer grounded in **real ICAR agronomic knowledge** through our retrieval system — and it reads the answer **back aloud** in their language. A farmer who can't read still gets expert advice."

> "And this answer isn't generic. The advisor already knows **his profile, his soil report, his active plan, his recent questions, and today's weather** — every reply is composed from *his* context."

*(Quickly show the **Soil Report OCR** on the profile page — upload or show an already-processed report.)*

> "Where does the soil intelligence come from? The farmer just **photographs a lab report** — like a blood test for the soil — and the AI **reads the pH, N-P-K and recommendations straight off the image.** No data entry, no agronomist visit. That report then personalises every fertiliser recommendation."

**👉 Problem solved:** *Closes the 1:1,000 extension gap — expert, context-aware advice, accessible by voice, in three languages, even to non-readers.*

**👉 Problems solved:** *Unclaimed government funds + true scale via WhatsApp/IVR/SMS.*

## 🎤 Q&A — Quick-reference cheat sheet (for the other 7 min)

- **"Is the data real?"** → Weather (Open-Meteo), mandi prices (Agmarknet/data.gov.in), schemes, and ICAR knowledge are **real/live**. AgriStack Farmer IDs, per-farmer Soil Health Card records, and Bharat-VISTAAR/NPSS pest feeds sit behind government partnerships — for those we use **clearly-labelled synthetic data** matching the real structure, swappable at production. Standard pre-partnership agritech approach.
- **"How is advice grounded — does it hallucinate?"** → RAG over an ICAR knowledge base in **Qdrant**; every chat/plan answer is composed from retrieved docs + the farmer's own profile, soil, plan, and live weather — not free-form generation.
- **"What's the tech stack?"** → Next.js 15 / React 19 / TypeScript; OpenAI for chat, Whisper STT, TTS, embeddings; Qdrant (RAG); AWS DynamoDB / S3 / SNS; Open-Meteo. JWT auth.
- **"How does it scale to millions?"** → Stateless per-request context assembly; advisory delivered via SMS/IVR (AWS SNS), so a smartphone isn't required. One deployment fans out to a whole state.
- **"Languages?"** → English, Hindi, Tamil today — UI, chat, voice in + voice out — architecture extends to Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Punjabi.
- **"Who pays / who's the customer?"** → State Ag departments & Bharat-VISTAAR (advisory layer partner), FPOs (16M farmers), agri-input firms, and agritech platforms needing a GenAI layer.
- **"What about no-literacy / no-smartphone farmers?"** → Voice-in/voice-out + daily SMS/IVR. The OCR means even soil data needs no typing.
- **"Business opportunity?"** → ₹990 Cr Digital Agriculture Mission allocation; agritech market ₹9B → ₹28B by 2030.
